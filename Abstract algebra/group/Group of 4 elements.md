(_Exploring group of 4 elements_)[]

Suppose [[Group]] $\langle G, \oplus \rangle$ is a group of order 4 ($char(G) = 4$).
Let $G = \{ 1, a, b, c \}$.

Explore options for $ab$ or $ba$:
$$
ba = \begin{cases}
1 \to a=b^{-1} \\
b \to a = 1 \\
c, \\
a \to b = 1 \
\end{cases}
\to ba = c 
$$
From another side we have also:
$$
ab = \begin{cases}
1 \to b=a^{-1} \\
b \to a = 1 \\
c, \\
a \to b = 1 \
\end{cases}
\to ab = c 
$$
Then for $a^{2}$:
$$
a^2 = \begin{cases}
1 \to a = 1 \\
b \\
c \\
a \to a = 1
\end{cases}
\to
a^2 = \begin{cases}
b \\
c
\end{cases}
$$
